A Traditional Catholic Liturgical Calendar for the Holy Sacrifice of the Mass according to the 1962 Missale Romanum. This app is designed for easy accessibility and increased awareness of the Traditional Mass. Features include:

  • iOS, watchOS & Widget Support
  • Liturgical Ordo
  • Prayers
  • Propers of the Mass
